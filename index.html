Our Story

10+ Years of Faith & Music

Bound for the Kingdom was built on a simple belief — that music has the power to move hearts, build community, and point people toward something greater. For over ten years, we've been living that out in Fulton, Illinois and surrounding areas.

From intimate songs & stories evenings to the outdoor stage at Riverview Bandshell, every event we host is crafted with care, purpose, and a deep love for the people who walk through the door.

Frequently Asked Questions

BFTK Fest 2026 runs Friday, September 4 through Saturday, September 5, 2026 at the Riverview Bandshell, 251 Ball Park Drive, Clinton, Iowa. Gates open Friday at 5:00 PM CDT and the festival runs through Saturday at 10:00 PM CDT.
A Night of Songs & Stories (Aug 1): GA $23.18 · VIP w/ Tenth Ave North $39.19 · Youth $17.85.

BFTK Fest (Aug 2–3): Student from $17.85 · Adult advance $28.52 · Prices rise after July 1.

All tickets available on Eventbrite. We recommend purchasing early — prices increase as the event approaches.
Friday, September 4: Jon Micah Sumrall of Kutless (headliner), Olivia Lane, Amanda Nolan, and Grace Heyvaert.

Saturday, September 5: Ryan Stevenson (headliner), Consumed By Fire, Madison Watkins, and Josh & Sarah Furrow.
Yes — BFTK Fest is a rain-or-shine event. We recommend bringing a rain poncho or light rain jacket. Weather updates will be communicated through our Facebook page and email list. Sign up below to stay informed.
Yes, lawn chairs and blankets are welcome at the Riverview Bandshell. Specific rules about chair height and setup areas will be confirmed closer to the event. Follow us on Facebook for updates.
